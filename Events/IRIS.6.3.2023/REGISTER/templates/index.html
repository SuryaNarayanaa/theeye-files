ABOUT

DATES : 4TH, 5TH AND 6TH MARCH 2022

This event will have 2 rounds.This is open to CSE, ECE, EEE, IT and AMCS Department Students of 1st, 2nd, 3rd and 4th Years.

Round 1 (Online)

Round 1 will have 2 streams: a Capture-The-Flag stream for ethical hackers and a Hackathon for application developers. The players are to choose from either stream and will have to solve their assigned problem(s) within the given time-frame from 10:00AM March 4 to 6:00PM March 5. Top 5 players from each stream will be selected for round 2.

Round 2 (Offline)

Finalists will pose as an Information Security Manager at an Indian bank and will face a randomly chosen incident from a list of bank-related cyber-incidents, and will have to write and submit a report to the judging panel. This event wil take place offline on March 6 from 4:30PM to 6:00PM.
