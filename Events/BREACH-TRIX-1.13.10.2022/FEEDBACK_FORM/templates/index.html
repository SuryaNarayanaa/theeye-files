BREACH 2022

ABOUT

DATES : 13TH AND 14TH OCTOBER, 2022

This Workshop will have 4 Sessions, split into 2 per day.This is only open to CSE Department Students of 2nd, 3rd and 4th Years. The workshop timings are from 4:30PM to 6:00PM.

Session 1

Session 1 will cover common IT Laws and real-life Analysis of Security Incidents and how to be safe and report cybercrimes. It will be a non-technical session.

Session 2

Session 2 will cover Technical Training with simulated CyberPhysicalSystems, Web Attacks, Viruses and Ransomware, etc. This will cover a brief on CPS/IoT/SCADA Hacking and XSS Attacks

Session 3

Session 3 will explain the networking protocol security.

Session 4

Session 3 will introduce the audiences to Dark Web and Dark Web Monitoring. It will also give a brief on Networking Models and Topography, and the security aspect in networks. It will also demonstrate some social engineering.
