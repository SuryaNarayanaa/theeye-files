Cyber Security
Workshop






Sessions

Session 1:

            Session 1 will cover com`n IT Laws and real-life Analysis of
            Security Incidents and how to be safe and report cybercrimes. It
            will be a non-technical session.
          
            20 Minutes :- IT Laws
            20 Minutes :- Why Cybersecurity is Important As an Individual
            10 Minutes :- A Sample Security Incident, and SOCs
            10 Minutes :- Q&A Session

Session 2:

            Session 2 will cover Technical Training with simulated
            CyberPhysicalSystems, Web Attacks, Viruses and Ransomware, etc. This
            will cover a brief on CPS/IoT/SCADA Hacking, XSS Attacks, and the
            Wireshark Tool.
          
            20 Minutes :- CPS/IoT/SCADA and a live attack on a simulated system
            10 Minutes :- XSS Attacks and a live sample XSS Attack
            10 Minutes :- the Wireshark Tool
            10 Minutes :- Viruses and Ransomware, A Ransomware Case Study
            10 Minutes :- Q&A Session

Session 3:

            Session 3 will introduce the audiences to Dark Web and Dark Web
            Monitoring. It will also give a brief on Networking Models and
            Topography, and the security aspect in networks. It will also
            demonstrate some social engineering.
          
            Minutes :- Proxy
            5 Minutes :- Dark Web and TOR
            5 Minutes :- DarkOwl
            5 Minutes :- Dark Web Monitoring
            10 Minutes :- OSI Model and TCP/IP Model
            10 Minutes :- HTTP vs HTTPS, Wireshark with live MITM attack
            10 Minutes :- ZPhisher and Phishing Attacks with sample live attack
            10 Minutes :- Q&A Session
